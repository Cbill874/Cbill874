About

  • 2001-2002

    My Beginnings

    I was born in Yiyang City, Hunan, China. When I was 13 months old, I was adopted from in Yiyang Children's Welfare Institution into a family with two older siblings.  

  • April 2008

     Born Again

    When I was old enough to understand that Jesus died on the cross, I  asked God to enter my heart and got saved. I was publicly baptized a year later.

  • March 2016

    Commitment

    I attended a conference where a man from China spoke. He was a part of the underground church, and after that night I made a commitment to do whatever job with God as the center and main focus of my life.

  • Present

    Into the Unknown

    God lead me to the Criminal Justice major, so it will be interesting to see what He has planned for the rest of my college life and throughout my future.
